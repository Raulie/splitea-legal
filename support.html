FAQ

How does receipt scanning work?

Point your camera at any receipt. Splitea uses AI to read the items and prices automatically — no manual entry needed.

Which payment apps are supported?

Splitea supports popular payment apps available in your region, including Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, Revolut, and Google Pay.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. Receipt images are processed in real-time and never stored. Your data stays on your device and syncs via iCloud.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You can cancel anytime before the next renewal period.

Can I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

Preguntas Frecuentes

¿Cómo funciona el escaneo de recibos?

Apunta tu cámara a cualquier recibo. Splitea usa IA para leer los artículos y precios automáticamente.

¿Qué aplicaciones de pago son compatibles?

Splitea es compatible con las aplicaciones de pago populares disponibles en tu región.
